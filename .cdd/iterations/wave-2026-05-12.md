## Protocol health signal

The wave pattern works. δ-as-agent is viable — tsc #30 proved γ can run full cycles autonomously via §5.2, and the cnos δ wave prompt successfully dispatched. But the infrastructure assumes single-cycle scope: artifacts, timeouts, and coordination surfaces are all cycle-scoped. Wave-level coordination is ad-hoc.

The three B-severity findings (#1, #2, #3) are the structural gap: CDD has no wave primitive. Single cycles are well-defined; sequences of cycles are stitched together by operator memory and /tmp files. This is the same class of gap #286 closed for α/β encapsulation — now at the δ/wave level.
