### Additional context (caught during Round 2) ‚Äî cross-Go-toolchain non-determinism

**Caught:** Round 2, while investigating why I3 was still red after Round 1's rebase + dist refresh.

**Surfaced:** `compress/gzip`'s flate output is **not byte-identical across minor Go toolchain versions.** CI was pinned to Go 1.22 (`go.mod` + three `setup-go@v5` calls); local dev was on Go 1.24.7. Same source tree produced different SHA-256s depending on which toolchain rebuilt. #264's `TestCreateTarGzDeterministic` only proved *within-version* determinism ‚Äî the cross-version axis was an unnamed assumption.

**Compounding defect on main:** I3 was already red on main at `07f021f` because `b4ca7e4`/`73de30b` landed SKILL.md edits (which live under `src/packages/cnos.cdd/`) without rebuilding dist. Even a rebuild by a matching toolchain would not have been authored in those commits.

**Fix:** Bumped `go.mod` `go 1.22` ‚Üí `go 1.24` and every `setup-go@v5` in `ci.yml`/`coherence.yml`/`release.yml` to `'1.24'`. Dist refreshed against matching toolchain. Landed in `24f12d4` (later subsumed by `da92e44`). Operator concurred on scope expansion before pushing.
